Better Rhetor Podcast: Political Platitudes

The Better Rhetor podcast strives to combat divisive political rhetoric by breaking it down and revealing toxic hidden meanings. Hopefully, it should serve as a reminder that politicians are trying to sway the audience toward their own identifiable team, that way they get voters who will hardly consider politicians from other parties. Voters need to think for themselves and decide on who they think would do best in office rather than which team, democrat or republican, they want to root for.
